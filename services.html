Services

Hosting and Domain Renewal

Your site hosting and domain name will be renewed automatically every year with no extra charge to you.

Minor Changes

Minor changes to your website are included at no extra cost. This includes changing images, fonts, etc. This feature is at our discretion to decide what is minor and what is not.

Hacked Site Recovery

No one plans to get hacked. But in the case of your site getting hacked, I'll restore it back to new as well as alter sensitive information to prevent another attack.

Database Backup

The database is the single most important aspect to a dynamic website. It stores usernames, passwords, dynamic content, edits, and a load of other stuff. Database Backup makes sure your site is always pulling the correct information to display and function properly.

Database Cleaning

A database is much like a hard drive. When things are constantly added and deleted, it can get jumbled and unorganized. We'll keep your database clean and organized to improve site speed and functionality.

Core Updates

This ensures your site is always running the latest updates and functioning with the best performance and highest security.

Plugin Updates

Plugins are a favorite target for hackers. When plugins get left without updating, hackers can easily launch an attack using the previous version's vulnerabilities. We'll keep your plugins up to date.

Performance Recommendations

We'll keep your site running fast by looking for ways to continue improve your site's speed and functionality. These are just recommendations, and it's always up to you if you want them implemented or not.

Site Performance Report

We'll send you a scheduled report on how your site has been doing, as well as what we have worked on during the scheduled time.

Video Tutorials

These video tutorials can walk you through all the aspects of your site and show you how everything works.
